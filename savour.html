Savour Fund
Savour is the Middle East’s first food vertical accelerator, helping startups in the food industry bring their
products and services to market at super-speed. It partners with early stage companies from across the food
value chain, whether the business is tech-focused or not.

Savour offers startups up to $100K Investment, expert mentorship, access to investors and their alumni
network, a leading curriculum, and coworking space.

Savour’s program curriculum was customized and curated by Creative Startups, a US-based accelerator
with 10 years’ experience in global markets. Startups that join Savour can immediately tap into a network of
established startups to accelerate their growth.
